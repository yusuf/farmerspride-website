## Introduction

Our service does not stop at the sale. We offer practical guidance, project support, and aftercare as a standard part of working with us — not an extra.

---

## Five Services

### 1. Product Guidance
Not sure which product is right for your situation? We help you choose — based on your crop, your growing conditions, and your specific need. Call us, describe the problem, and we will give you a direct answer.

### 2. Landscaping Support
We supply plants, growing media, fertilizers, and tools for landscaping projects — and we can advise on what works in Maldivian outdoor conditions. Available for resort grounds teams, contractors, and private projects.

### 3. After-Sales Support
Bought a product and have a follow-up question? We are available. Call, WhatsApp, or Viber. We do not consider the sale closed until the product is working correctly for you.

### 4. Supply for Projects
For larger orders, ongoing supply contracts, or project-based requirements, we can discuss lead times, quantities, and scheduling directly. Contact us to discuss your project needs.

### 5. Phone & Messaging Assistance
Our primary service channel is direct contact. Phone for fast advice and ordering. WhatsApp or Viber for photos, follow-ups, and product confirmations. Email for formal documentation and purchase orders.
