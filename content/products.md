## Individual Product Listing Template

Each product entry must contain these fields:

- **Product name:** [Brand] [Product Name] — [Descriptor] | [Size/Weight]
  Example: *Yara Fertilizer — High Potassium | 50kg*
- **Brand:** manufacturer name
- **Size / Weight:** always stated explicitly
- **Usage description:** one sentence — what it is for, when to use it
- **Availability:** In Stock / Pre-Order / Coming Soon
- **Price:** MVR [amount] or "Contact for Price"
- **Buttons:** Call to Order  |  WhatsApp to Ask
